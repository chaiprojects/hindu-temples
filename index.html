About

This website serves as a centralized, convenient reference for Hindu temples in the San Francisco Bay Area, covering the corridor from Concord through Oakland, Fremont, Tri-Valley, and Milpitas down to San Jose. It brings together essential information for devotees: temple locations on an interactive map, darshan timings, daily Rahu Kalam calculations, Ekadashi dates, and upcoming festivals and events — all in one place.

Whether you are new to the Bay Area or a longtime resident, this resource helps you find the right temple, plan your visits around auspicious timings, and stay informed about community celebrations across the region.

Disclaimer

Temple timings and festival dates can change without notice. Users should always verify current information with the official temple website or by calling ahead, especially during festival seasons when special schedules may apply.

Rahu Kalam and Ekadashi calculations are provided on a best-effort basis using astronomical algorithms (NOAA solar position and Jean Meeus lunar calculations). Results may differ slightly from traditional panchang calculations or regional customs. For authoritative guidance, please consult your preferred priest or panchang.

Map coordinates are approximate. Festival dates listed here follow commonly observed North/South Indian traditions and may vary by sampradaya or region.
